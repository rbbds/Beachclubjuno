Beachclub Juno Website

Design a full website for Beachclub Juno in Figma. Use the brand assets and style guidelines below. The design should feel warm, laid-back, and coastal — with a cultural edge. Think of the vibe of blow.surf: editorial layout, bold type, generous whitespace, large atmospheric photography, smooth scroll sections. But grounded in Juno's own brand identity.

Brand identity
Logo
The Juno wordmark uses a rounded, bold custom display typeface (chunky, friendly, slightly retro). Beneath the wordmark is a signature wavy line element — a decorative "golf" (wave) used as a recurring graphic motif throughout the design. Use this wave as a section divider, decorative underline, or background texture element. Logo and wave assets will be delivered as SVG and PNG.

Visual style rules

Backgrounds: Use cream #f6f4db as the default page background. Use navy #3d7183 for dark-mode accent sections (e.g. footer, quote blocks).
Buttons: Primary = terracotta fill #cc6435, white text, rounded corners (8px). Secondary = outlined in navy.
Wave motif: Use the Juno wave SVG (or approximate with a path) as a decorative element under section titles, as a horizontal divider, or as a subtle background watermark. It should appear on every page.
Photography style: Placeholder frames should indicate: golden hour beach light, candid social scenes, food close-ups, stage/performance shots. Warm, slightly desaturated, film-like.
Spacing system: 8px base grid. Section padding 80px top/bottom. Card padding 24px.
Corner radius: Cards and image frames: 12px. Buttons: 8px.
No harsh drop shadows — use very subtle elevation (2–4px soft shadow, low opacity) or none.
Cultural, not clubby — avoid anything that reads as young/party. The audience is 35–65, culturally engaged, appreciates quality and taste.
